Hello world!

I am a Ph.D. student at Birkbeck, University of London in the Department of Computer Science and Information Systems and the Department of Psychological Sciences, advised by Geroge Magoulas and Michael Thomas. My current research orbits around machine learning and behavioural genetics with a particular interest in transfer of learning (in deep neural networks), evolutionary algorithms and population studies.

In the dept. of computer science I am a member of the Birkbeck Knowledge Lab, Birkbeck Institute for Data Analytics and the Experimental Data Science Research Group.

At the Centre for Brain & Cognitive Development I am a member of the Developmental Neurocognition Lab and the Birkbeck Babylab.

I am also teaching Introduction to Data Analytics and Machine Learning with Python at City, University of London.
